Wellness Tips

  • Stay hydrated before and after your spa treatments to maximize benefits.
  • Arrive early to your appointment to relax and prepare for your treatment.
  • Communicate with your therapist about any preferences or concerns.
  • Take deep breaths and focus on relaxation during your treatment.
  • Avoid heavy meals and alcohol before your spa visit.
  • Take your time to enjoy the post-treatment relaxation area.
